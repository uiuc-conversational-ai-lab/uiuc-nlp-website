Zea Mays Center for Computational Linguistics
at the University of Illinois

      

Computational language research at U. Illinois is spread out over a number of departments, and covers a wide range of topics. Please see the individual faculty and group home pages (below) for the details. This central web site is relatively new. Please send additional links (e.g. faculty, research groups) to mfleck@illinois.edu.
